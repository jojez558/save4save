2. Privacy Policy

2.1 What We Collect

  • 📝 Full Name — for identification in the contact list
  • 📱 WhatsApp Number — the whole point of this platform lol
  • 📸 Your Photo — stored locally in your browser (not on any external server)
  • 🏛️ Institution, Course, Year — optional but helps identify you in the network
  • ⏰ Date Registered — for record keeping only

2.2 How We Use It

  • ✅ To generate your personal VCF contact file
  • ✅ To allow the admin to manage and share contact lists with group members
  • ❌ We will NEVER sell your data to anyone. Deadass never. 🙅‍♂️
  • ❌ We do not use your data for advertisements

2.3 Where Data Lives

All data is stored in your browser's localStorage — this means it lives on YOUR device, not on any external server. If you clear your browser data, contacts are gone too. That's why downloading your VCF is important bestie! Always backup fr fr. 💾

2.4 Data Sharing

By saving your contact on Save4Save, you agree that your name and phone number may be included in VCF files shared with other group members by the admin. That's literally the whole point — connecting people. If you don't want to be shared, don't sign up. Simple! 🤷

2.5 Your Rights

  • ✉️ Right to be Removed: DM admin on WhatsApp to request deletion of your contact
  • 🔍 Right to Access: You can view your data in the downloaded VCF
  • ✏️ Right to Correct: Contact admin to fix any mistakes in your info
